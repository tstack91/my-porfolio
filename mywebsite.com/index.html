About Me

I'm a married dad of a golden retriever currently working at Amazon as an Onsite Medical Representative. I started in this role because of my licenses and certifications in Healthcare and Emergency Medical Services. I've spent the last few years working on an ambulance in a commercial EMS system. I started prior to the Covid-19 pandemic and worked through the first couple years of the pandemic until the situation seemed contained. After Covid-19 was in endemic status and feeling as though I had completed my service to my community, I started looking at options on how to return to school through loans, programs or other employers. Fast forward to now and life is a bit slower albeit more chaotic. I work a full time position at Amazon while attending Correlation One's Software developer course. In any spare time I like to hike and play tennis with my wife and dog, I'm also an avid gamer and content creator on Twitch.

I created this page to showcase my HTML capabilities for Project 2 of the Correlation One course, you can find links to more information about me throughout the page. My journey to becoming a software developer

I started the Software Developer course in the beginning of April 2023 hoping to break into the tech field. I wanted to work in programming and game design ever since I was a young boy who excelled at math, especially logic and reasoning puzzles as I always loved brain teasers. I eventually started down other career paths but circled back to it being a possibility after starting to use excel last year and loving all of the functions I could put together. Everything was a puzzle with an overlaying UI but it was amazing to see the kind of automation you could create behind the scenes with macros and functions. I started poking around to see what programs Amazon offered, at the time the Amazon Technical Academy (ATA) existed but was not taking new applicants. I continued to dig around until I found the Career Choice program through which I would start this program. At first I wondered if I would be good enough, hoped I made the right choice. To my pleasure, everything continued to click and I knew this is where I was meant to be. Less than a month into the program I began setting up my next challenge, obtaining my BS in a computer science field. I've enrolled with Southern New Hampshire University's online program as they are said to have one of the best Computer Science programs available online.
